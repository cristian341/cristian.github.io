Wedding Services

The following guide on services, packages and pricing have been created to share ideas of when may wish to enjoy a live session pianist during your Wedding day.
These have been based on Ben’s previous experiences however every Wedding is personal and unique. Feel free to reach out to Ben, who would love to listen to your
ideas and everything you wish for on your special day. He can also help to provide a free personalised, no obligation quote.

Services

The Marriage Ceremony (£250)

Your special music selection performed beautifully as guests arrive to take their seats (5 songs/music pieces), as you walk down the aisle (1 song/music piece), sign the register (2 songs/music pieces) and exit (1 song/music piece).

The Wedding Breakfast (£300)

Live piano instrumental performance of your chosen playlist of music. The perfect accompaniment as you & loved ones enjoy the Wedding meal. Approximately 90 minutes of performance, split either 2 x 45 or 3 x 30 minute sets.

The Drinks Reception (£275)

Live piano instrumental music performed whilst you enjoy celebratory drinks with your family and friends. Decide a personal playlist of music which Ben can perform beautiful renditions too. Approximately 75 minutes of performance.

The Evening Spectacle (£250)

Personalised arrangements to your music choices to time perfectly with the choreography and timing or your First Dance, as well as beautiful renditions to your chosen music for the Wedding Cake cutting. 1 hour of performance.

Combo Package offer

Offering even better value, performances can be booked as a combined package. Examples listed are popular combinations Ben has provided for Wedding couples previously:
  1. The Marriage Ceremony & Drinks Reception (£375)
  2. The Marriage Ceremony & Wedding Breakfast (£425
  3. The Drinks Reception & Wedding Breakfast (£425)
  4. The Drinks Reception & Evening Spectacle (£400)
  5. The Full day covering all services: Marriage Ceremony, Drinks Reception, Wedding Breakfast & Evening Spectacle (£545)
(Kindly note, pricing includes Ben’s travel within a 50 mile area from his home in South Derbyshire, UK. For Weddings taking place further afield in the UK or overseas, Ben would be happy to talk together with you to create a free, no obligation personalised quote)

Tailored to what you love

Performances are customised to meet to your specific requests, timings and arrangements. Ben would love to hear about your ideas and would welcome you to to contact Ben for a free personalised, no obligation quote.
