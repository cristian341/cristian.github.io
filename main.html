Ben Chay

Midlands-based pianist providing a touch of elegance to your special day. The piano is possibly the most versatile, offering an incredible range unlike any other instrument with an ability to play several different tones simultaneously. The piano can be romantic, emotional, sophisticated, yet a great deal of fun and entertainment to your day. With over 20 years of performance experience, Ben’s repertoire is extensive covering music from Classical and Jazz, to more contemporary fields including Film, Disney, Musicals and Western/Eastern popular music. As a musician, Ben began performances in the Classical music arena in 2001, performing across the UK in venues which include the Royal Albert Hall & Royal Festival Hall in London, Birmingham Symphony Hall, along with others in Buckinghamshire, Cambridgeshire, Derbyshire, Greater Manchester, Hampshire, Leicestershire, Northamptonshire and Somerset. His performances have taken him overseas also, playing to audiences in Austria (Salzburg), Italy (northern region of Val D’Aosta and southern region of Puglia) and Malaysia (Kuala Lumpur).
